Amsterdamse Klanken

Viusal Design - 20 December, 2023.

In this Content course, the group was tasked to make a podcast, regarding the view of Amsterdam from the eyes of three students, including myself! Around the podcast, we had to make merchandise but also a Medium Fidelity website. Even though, the podcast is a private podcast, I still have the website in Figma.

During the course, we learned how important tone of voice, knowledge of your target audience is and how to build up a brand. We practiced with content and storytelling but it was also a reminder of how important it is how you or your company represents themselves at other people. After all, a very good learning lesson.
