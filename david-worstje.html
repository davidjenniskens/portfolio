David's Worstje

Viusal Design - 21 December, 2023.

In our very first Design course called Vormgeving, I was tasked to make an onepage website on my favorite food. In which I was appointed the visual style called Naive Mascots. Naive Mascots are characterized by a simple or childlike visual style. These mascots often have a friendly and approachable appearance, typically with exaggerated features or simple designs that make them easily recognizable and memorable. Naive Mascots are often used to create a sense of trustworthiness, reliability, and approachability. They appeal to audiences of all ages and can be effective in promoting brand recognition and loyalty.

During the course, we focused on working with colour, form, and typography. But also the Gestalt Principles and the principles of design. How to make one cohesive lay-out impleting the following laws: the law of simplicity, the law of fore and background and the law of proximity.
